# Research Scope: AI-Assisted Software Development Workflow Metrics

**Date:** 2026-04-11
**Profile:** medium
**Goals:** Understand what metrics are measurable from session logs (tool calls, errors, completions, retries) for detecting regressions or improvements in AI-driven agentic engineering workflows like Momentum (sprint planning, story implementation, code review, retrospectives driven by AI agents).

## Sub-Questions

1. What metrics do GitHub Copilot, Cursor, and similar AI coding tools expose to teams, and how are they computed from session telemetry?
2. How have DORA metrics (lead time, deployment frequency, change failure rate, MTTR) been adapted for AI-driven development workflows?
3. What AI workflow quality metrics are practitioners actually tracking — acceptance rate, iteration count, prompt refinement cycles, context efficiency — and how are they computed from logs?
4. What does AI practice maturity measurement look like — how do teams measure whether their prompting and workflow practices are improving over time?
5. What is the state of regression detection in AI workflows — how do teams detect when a practice change made quality worse, using observable session data?
