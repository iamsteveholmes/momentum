Seven phases on a continuous loop. Forward flow is the dominant motion, but feedback flows freely between many phases — the wheel doesn't require strict sequential completion. Each rotation refines the practice itself, compounding over time.

Feedback patterns (not strict)

  • Verification fail → Execution — story flunks E2E, dev re-runs in this sprint.
  • Verification fail → Capture — fix is too big; queue as new story for a future sprint.
  • Retro findings → Triage / Distill — observations get classified or directly applied to rules/skills.
  • Distill outputs → all phases — practice updates show up in better Discovery, Grooming, Execution next time.
  • Anything post-Capture → Discovery — open questions surface that demand new research or assessment.
  • Forward arrows show the dominant flow; feedback can short-circuit at any point.
